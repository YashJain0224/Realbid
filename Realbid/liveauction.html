Auction Rules

  • The property will be opened for bidding at the mentioned time.
  • The bidding will be stopped when the auction duration is over or only one participant is left in the room.
  • The bidder has to pay the auction fees before bidding and the fees will be refunded if you didn't win.
  • The participant who won the auction has to pay the certain price of the property which he won.
  • The participant can place any number of bids.
  • Extra time will be awarded if constant bids are placed.
  • The details of the owner of the property will be given to the participant who won the auction.
